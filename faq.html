Cleaning Services

Our standard cleaning includes dusting all surfaces, vacuuming carpets, mopping hard floors, cleaning bathrooms (toilets, showers, sinks), kitchen cleaning (countertops, sink, appliance exteriors), and emptying trash. We use eco-friendly cleaning products and pay special attention to high-touch areas.
