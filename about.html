Who We Are

Tipgee is a professional cleaning and maid allocation service dedicated to transforming homes and workplaces into pristine, healthy environments.

Our mission is to provide exceptional cleaning services through well-trained, trustworthy staff who treat your space with the utmost care and respect.

Founded in 2015, we've grown from a local cleaning service to a trusted provider of comprehensive cleaning solutions. All our cleaners undergo rigorous background checks and training to ensure the highest standards of service and reliability. We take pride in our eco-friendly cleaning products and customer-first approach.
